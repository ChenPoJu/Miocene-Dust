Figure 7. Spatial patterns of net TOA radiation anomalies. 
Results from sensitivity experiments examining (a) Miocene paleogeographic and land surface ERF, 
(b) doubling-CO2 ERF, and (c-d) Miocene dust ERFs. (a) shows the difference between fSST_Mio.SSTPi.DustPi and fSST_Pi.DustPi. 
(b) displays the comparison between fSST_Pi.560CO2 and fSST_Pi.280CO2. (c-d) are identical to Figure 3a & 4a, respectively, 
but are replotted using a common color bar with (b) to facilitate direct comparison. Notably, (b-d) share a consistent color cale,  
which differs from the scale used in (a). The grey and black lines denote the preindustrial and Middle Miocene land outlines, respectively. 
The global mean values (GM) are shown in top-right of each panel and correspond to the ERF values shown in Table S1. 
The values include uncertainties which are one-standard-deviation of the interannual variability in the global mean value over the last 25 simulation years.
