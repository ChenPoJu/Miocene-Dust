Figure 2. Climate state anomalies (MMio minus PiCtrl). 
(a) surface temperature, (b) inward net radiation at TOA, (c) total dust concentration in the atmosphere, 
and (d) total dust deposition rate to the surface. The global mean values (GM) are shown in top-right of each panel, 
and the percentages are the increases or decreases of MMio’s GM values comparedto the PiCtrl. 
The black and grey lines in the panels (a-d) denote the Middle Miocene and preindustrial continental outlines, respectively. 
The bottom panels show the changes in sea-ice fractions in (e) the north pole and (f) the south pole. 
The grey mask denotes the Middle Miocene lands, and the black lines are the preindustrial land outlines.
