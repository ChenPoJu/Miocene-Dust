Figure 1. The spatial distribution of (a) dominant plant type, bare ground, and land ice, 
(b) soil erodibility factor S, and (c) surface dust emission in PiCtrl (left) and MMio (right). 
The dominant plant type shows regimes where a plant function type (PFT) has the highest percentage 
among other possible PFTs within a grid cell (NE: Needleleaf Evergreen; BE: Broadleaf Evergreen; BD: Broadleaf Deciduous). 
Zero values indicate grid cells dominated by bare ground, and seventeen indicate grid cells dominated by land ice.
